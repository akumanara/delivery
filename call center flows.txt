Store with our app and app is online
---
Order is sent to delivery.gr
Order is sent to app on store premises
Store accepted order in less than 2 mins and gave estimation delivery time 25mins



Store with our app and app is online
---
Order is sent to delivery.gr
Order is sent to app on store premises
After 2 mins the order is sent to the call center to ping the store
Call center holds the order in less than 4 mins
Order is accepted via call center or via store app



Store with our app and app is online
---
Order is sent to delivery.gr
Order is sent to app on store premises
After 2 mins the order is sent to the call center to ping the store
Call center is unavailabe order is rejected (4 mins passed)



Store with our app and app is online
---
Order is sent to delivery.gr
Order is sent to app on store premises
After 2 mins the order is sent to the call center to ping the store
Call center holds the order
Call center is unavailabe to find the store so the order is rejected



Store without our app
---
Order is sent to delivery.gr
Order is sent to call center
Call center holds the order in less than 9 mins
Call center accepts the order with estimated delivery time from the store (ie. 25mins)


Store without our app
---
Order is sent to delivery.gr
Order is sent to call center
